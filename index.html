Branches of the Heart

On the night a choice can’t be postponed any longer, a Singaporean engineer lies awake with his phone face-down—five names pressing into a single decision. His memories play out of order: a brilliant boss who respects competence like truth, a preschool teacher whose warmth feels like home, a Kazakh traveller in Sapa who turns mist into permanence, a close friend who knows his patterns and still chooses gentleness, and an ex-nurse he once loved—re-met by fate on a sacred journey in Mecca.
In a story built on branching choices, every moment shifts three invisible meters—Affection, Trust, and Tension—and the ending isn’t decided by love alone, but by whether he becomes someone safe to choose.
